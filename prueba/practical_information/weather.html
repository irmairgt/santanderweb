Weather in Santander

Your guide to the current weather and seasonal climate of this coastal gem.

Current Weather

Temperature: 18°C

Condition: Partly Cloudy

Humidity: 75%

Seasonal Climate

Spring

Fresh temperatures around 15°C with occasional showers. Perfect for exploring Santander’s parks.

Summer

Warm and sunny, averaging 24°C. Ideal for beach lovers and outdoor activities.

Autumn

Mild weather with highs around 20°C. A mix of sunny and rainy days creates stunning coastal views.

Winter

Crisp and cool, averaging 10°C. Rainy spells are common but coastal beauty remains.
