GRAN HOTEL SARDINERO
This seafront hotel is 20 metres from Sardinero Beach. It offers elegant rooms, many with views of the Cantabrian Sea, 20 metres from the Sardinero Gran Casino.

HOTEL BAHIA
Overlooking Santander Bay and the city's cathedral, Hotel Bahía offers free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour reception. Santander Ferry Terminal and the main train and bus stations are just 200 metres away.

HOTEL HOYUELA
This luxurious 4-star hotel is located on the beach, just 400 m from the Palacio de la Magdalena. It is within walking distance of Santander city centre and the university.

EUROSTARS HOTEL REAL
This impressive palace-style building offers beautiful views of Santander Bay and Sardinero Beach. It features a free gym and elegant, classic-style rooms with free Wi-Fi.

HOSTERIA SANTANDER
A more affordable and cozy option near the city center, with simple yet comfortable facilities. Its proximity to restaurants and points of interest makes it an excellent choice for practical travelers.

HOTEL CHIQUI
Hotel Chiqui is Known for its modern facilities, it features comfortable rooms, an elegant restaurant serving local cuisine, and excellent service. The hotel's location makes it ideal for enjoying the coastline and exploring Santander's main attractions.
