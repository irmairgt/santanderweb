RABAS
Rabas from Santander are a traditional dish from Cantabria, consisting of squid rings battered and fried until crispy. They are typically served as a tapa or appetizer, accompanied by lemon and often enjoyed with a refreshing drink.

COCIDO MONTAÑÉS
Cocido Montañés is a hearty traditional stew from Cantabria, made with white beans, pork, cabbage, and chorizo. It's a comforting dish perfect for cold weather, symbolizing the region's rural cuisine.

SOBAOS PASIEGOS
Sobaos Pasiegos are a traditional pastry from Cantabria, made with butter, flour, sugar, and eggs. These sweet treats are known for their rich, buttery flavor and soft texture, often enjoyed with coffee or milk.

QUESADA PASIEGA
Quesada Pasiega is a traditional dessert from Cantabria, made with fresh cheese, eggs, sugar, and flour. Its creamy and slightly tangy flavor makes it a beloved regional specialty.

ANCHOAS DE SANTOÑA
Anchoas de Santoña are world-famous Cantabrian anchovies, known for their exquisite flavor and delicate texture. They are carefully hand-processed and preserved in olive oil, representing a hallmark of the region's seafood delicacies.

CABRITO AL HORNO
Cabrito al Horno is a traditional Cantabrian dish featuring tender goat meat roasted to perfection. It is seasoned with aromatic herbs and accompanied by roasted potatoes or vegetables, showcasing the region's rustic cuisine.
