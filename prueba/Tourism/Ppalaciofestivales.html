Palacio de Festivales

The Palacio de Festivales of Santander is a major cultural venue located in the city of Santander, Spain. Inaugurated in 1991, it was designed by architect *Juan Navarro Baldeweg* and is one of the city's most important centers for the performing arts. The building hosts a wide variety of events, including concerts, theater performances, ballet, opera, and festivals. It consists of several auditoriums and halls, including the main *Cotilla Hall*, which is known for its excellent acoustics.

The Palacio de Festivales is also home to the **Festival Internacional de Santander**, one of the most prestigious cultural events in northern Spain.
