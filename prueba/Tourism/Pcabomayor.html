Cabo Mayor

Cabo Mayor is a prominent cape located on the northern coast of Santander, Spain. It is known for its stunning natural beauty and its iconic Cabo Mayor Lighthouse, which has been guiding ships since 1839. The area is a popular spot for both locals and visitors, offering breathtaking views of the Bay of Santander and the Cantabrian Sea.


Cabo Mayor features rugged cliffs, walking paths, and green spaces, making it ideal for hiking and outdoor activities. The surrounding park and coastal environment are perfect for enjoying nature and observing local wildlife.
