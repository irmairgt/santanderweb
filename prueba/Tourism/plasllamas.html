Las Llamas

The Parque de las Llamas is a large public park located in the city of Santander, Spain. It covers an area of approximately 200,000 square meters and is one of the city's most important green spaces. The park features a variety of walking paths, gardens, ponds, and recreational areas, making it a popular spot for locals and visitors to relax, exercise, and enjoy nature. It was designed with modern urban planning principles and includes playgrounds, sports facilities, and spaces for outdoor events.


The park is also home to the Centro de Educación Ambiental (Environmental Education Center), where visitors can learn about sustainability and local ecology. With its mix of natural beauty and urban design, the Parque de las Llamas is an essential part of Santander’s landscape and a key space for leisure and community activities.
