Bay

La Bahía de Santander is a natural harbor located in the city of Santander, Cantabria, Spain. It is one of the most scenic and important features of the city, offering stunning views of the surrounding coastline and the Cantabrian Sea. The bay is protected by the Peninsula de la Magdalena and the Península de la Escondida, making it a calm and sheltered body of water. The area is popular for boating, sailing, and walking along the waterfront promenades.


It is also home to various beaches, such as Playa de los Peligros and Playa de la Magdalena, which attract both locals and tourists. The bay is a key part of Santander’s identity, serving as a hub for maritime activities, tourism, and local culture. Its picturesque setting and tranquil waters make it a top destination for visitors looking to enjoy the natural beauty of the region.
