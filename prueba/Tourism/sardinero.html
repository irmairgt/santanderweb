Sardinero

Playa del Sardinero is one of the most famous and popular beaches in Santander, Cantabria. Located in a wide bay, it is known for its golden sand and calm waters, making it ideal for a day of sun and sea. The beach is close to the city center, which makes it easily accessible and lively, especially during the summer.


It offers excellent facilities, such as nearby restaurants and shops. The beach is also famous for its vibrant atmosphere and its beautiful promenade, which provides stunning views of the sea.
