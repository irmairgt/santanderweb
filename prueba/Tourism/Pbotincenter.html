Botin Center

The Centro Botín is a contemporary art center located in Santander, Cantabria, Spain. Designed by the renowned architect Renzo Piano, the building was inaugurated in 2017 and is situated on the waterfront of the city's bay. The center is dedicated to the promotion and exhibition of modern and contemporary art, with a strong focus on international and Spanish artists. It hosts temporary exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events.


The architecture of the building is striking, featuring two white, curved pavilions connected by a central core and large glass windows offering panoramic views of the sea. The Centro Botín is also home to a public space, including a café and a rooftop terrace, making it a popular destination for both art lovers and visitors seeking stunning views. The center is part of the Botín Foundation’s commitment to cultural development in the region and has quickly become an important cultural landmark in Santander.
