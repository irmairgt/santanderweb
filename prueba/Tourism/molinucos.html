Molinucos

Playa de los Molinucos is a small, peaceful beach located in Santander, Cantabria. Known for its natural beauty, it is tucked away in a more secluded area, offering a tranquil atmosphere ideal for those seeking a quiet escape.


The beach features fine sand and clear waters, making it perfect for a relaxing day by the sea. Surrounded by cliffs and lush vegetation, it provides a picturesque setting.


It is also less crowded than other beaches in the area, providing a more intimate and serene environment, particularly outside of the peak summer season.
