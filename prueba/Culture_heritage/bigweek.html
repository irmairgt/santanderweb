Big week

The *Big Week of Santander* is one of the most anticipated festivals of the year in the city, and for good reason! It takes place in *August* and is a celebration full of joy, music, and events for all tastes. During these days, Santander becomes a huge outdoor stage with concerts, performances, and activities filling the squares and streets.

One of the highlights of the event is the *parade of floats*, with giant, beautifully decorated floats and participants dressed in dazzling costumes that amaze the spectators. There are also *fairgrounds* with fun *rides* and *attractions* for all ages, creating a festive atmosphere throughout the city.

And of course, the *fireworks* over Santander's bay are a tradition you can't miss. Each night, the sky is lit up with impressive pyrotechnic displays, creating a magical atmosphere that captivates everyone in the city.

In short, the *Big Week* is a celebration that blends tradition and modernity, where the streets of Santander come alive with life and fun. In 2024, it will be celebrated in the last week of *August*, making it the perfect time to enjoy everything this coastal city has to offer!
