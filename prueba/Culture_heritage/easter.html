Easter week

Easter in Santander is one of the most important festivities in the city, full of emotion and devotion. During the week, the streets transform into a stage for solemn processions, where brotherhoods parade with floats depicting scenes from the Passion of Christ. The most notable processions are the Procession of the Holy Burial on Holy Thursday, and the Flagellation Procession, which travels through the main streets of the city center.

Each float is accompanied by the sound of drums and cornets, creating a deeply religious atmosphere. Additionally, the city fills with fervor and reverence, with thousands of people following the processions on foot, and some even actively participating as members of the brotherhoods.

Easter in Santander is not only a liturgical event but also a cultural manifestation that connects the people of Santander with their deepest traditions. In 2024, Easter will take place from March 24 to 31, with the main celebrations between Holy Thursday and Easter Sunday. It's a unique experience you can't miss!
